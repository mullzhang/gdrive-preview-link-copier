# Privacy Policy

Google Drive Preview Link Copier processes supported Google Drive, Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Drive links locally in the browser.

The extension may read clipboard text only when the user invokes a copy or conversion action, and may write the converted Google Drive preview URL back to the clipboard.

The extension stores only the user's language preference.

The extension does not transmit, store, sell, or share user data with external servers or third parties.
